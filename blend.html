See the Near Future
Fast Forward Labs helps organizations accelerate their data science and machine intelligence capabilities. Every quarter we profile a different near future technology, delivering a report on its development and a prototype demonstrating its application.
Our reports include an overview of the state and trajectory of each technology in the academic, startup, and commercial spaces.
Our prototypes show the technology in action, and provide an understanding of the engineering constraints and opportunities.
