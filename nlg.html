Natural Language Generation Report and Prototype

Natural Language Generation is a technique that allows software systems to write articles. These capabilities are being used by everyone from the Associate Press, to Yahoo Fantasy Sports to banks that just want to make numerical data friendly and easy to understand. We expect to see even more applications emerge in the next year.

Our report and prototype for natural language generation will show you how these systems work, and how to build your own. This report is now on sale as an stand-alone report.

Natural Language Generation

Our report includes an overview of the state and trajectory of natural language generation in the academic, startup, and commercial spaces.

Roborealtor

See natural language generation in action in our prototype, RoboRealtor. Inspired by real estate sites, RoboRealtor takes the attributes for a hypothetical apartment and generates listings.
