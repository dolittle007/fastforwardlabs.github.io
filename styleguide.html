Colors
Our standard color scheme is a white background, black text, and a cyan accent color. On the web, we use cyan as a link color.
Cyan
Hex Code: #00dcec
Fonts
FF Chamber Sans
FF Chambers sans is our logo font. We also use it for headers and captions in our reports. It gets tiring to read in long paragraphs so use it carefully.
FF Tisa
Our reports are set in FF Tisa. It is good for body text, especially for printed documents.
FF Tisa Sans
Our web body text is set in FF Tisa Sans. I might try and change it soon, though.
