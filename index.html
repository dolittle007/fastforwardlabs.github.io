About the Tech Graph

The tech graph is our digital whiteboard. A place where we track technologies we are interested in and the connections between them. Some of these ideas will go on to become full reports, others newsletter dispatches, and still others hack day projects. In all cases, thinking about and sharing them is an integral part of our research process.

How We Work

We believe the only way to really understand a technology is to build with it. Our prototypes show the technology in action, and provide a deep understanding of the engineering constraints and opportunities it offers. Our reports include an overview of the state and trajectory of each technology in the academic, startup, and commercial spaces. Taken together, they present a vision of both the algorithms of the near future and the products they'll power.
