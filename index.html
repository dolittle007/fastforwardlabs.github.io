This is where we keep some of demos and static assets.

Demos

  • Brief Preview - A public preview version of our summarization prototype Brief.
  • Probabilistic Real Estate - A probabilistic programming prototype that predicts future real estate prices across the New York City boroughs.
  • Luhn Method Demo - An interactive demonstration of using the Luhn method for summarization.
  • Visualizing the Taste of a Community of Cinephiles Using t-SNE - an interactive visualization that clusters movies together based on user ratings.

Resources

  • Visuals - Media files (images, gifs, video) for each of our report and prototype releases. URLs are stable so you can use them to embed.
  • Prototype videos - A video player that plays the demos for all our prototypes in order. Good for projecting at at events.
