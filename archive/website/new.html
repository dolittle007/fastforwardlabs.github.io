Fast Forward Labs helps organizations accelerate their data science and machine intelligence capabilities. Every quarter we profile a different near future technology, producing a report on its development and a prototype demonstrating its application.
Reports include an overview of the state and trajectory of each technology in the academic, startup, and commercial spaces.
Prototypes show the technology in action, and provide a deep understanding of the engineering constraints and opportunities it offers.
