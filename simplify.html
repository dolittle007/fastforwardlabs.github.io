A Better Process

Fast Forward Labs was born out of a desire to fix a broken research process. To bridge the gap between start-ups, large companies and academia, and to move beyond a consulting model. We have designed each step in our process to empower our clients, giving them the info and the perspective necessary to best plot their own course into the future.

Prototyping

At the center of our process is prototyping. By putting our selected tech to the test in minimum viable product prototypes we build a robust understanding of their possibilities and limitations, wrestling with implementation realities and squeezing out any theoretical hand-waviness.

Reporting

The prototypes feed our reports, where we record the lessons we learned and situate the technology within the broader landscape. Concise yet rigorous, our reports provide you with everything you need to know about what’s coming around the corner.

Advising

Our client advising sharpens our reporting, fleshing out specific implementations relevant to the client’s interests. It also completes the virtuous circle of our process, as our clients concerns point us toward the places to concentrate. Because our subscribers span a wide range of industries we are able to observe patterns and needs across interests, giving us a broad perspective that we then share back with our client organizations.

How We Choose Our Topics

This is a question we get asked a lot and one we love to answer. It’s also a central part of our value to our clients.

We sit our office and drink lots of coffee and come up with as many bad ideas as we can. Our prep work is reading good and bad sci-fi, reading academic papers for fun, building face morphing systems to run on each other, creating pulley systems to get our whiteboard up to the fifth floor. Engaging with our clients and their concerns. Just being weird people who like this stuff a lot.

It is a process we have a lot of fun with and one we take seriously. One of the reasons we are not a consulting company is that we believe in our ability to deliver more value by choosing the topics we work on. So that instead of playing catch-up you’re confident in your understanding and ability to make the most of recent developments.
