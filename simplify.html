Research, Applied

Fast Forward Labs was founded to provide a new and better research model. We have designed each step in our process to empower our clients, giving them the information and perspective necessary to best plot their own course into the future.

Prototyping

Prototyping is at the heart of our process. By putting the tech to the test in minimum viable product prototypes, we build a deep understanding of its possibilities and limitations.

Reporting

The prototypes feed our reports, where we record lessons we learned and situate the technology within the broader landscape. Concise yet rigorous, our reports provide you with everything you need to know about the topic.

Advising

We supplement the product with client advising, helping companies apply the capabilities to their specific business problems. Advising also completes the virtuous circle, as clients concerns inspire future research topics.

Work With Us

We operate on an annual subscription model. Clients receive quarterly reports, access to prototypes, and a set number of advising hours.

We work with clients across multiple industries, including insurance, publishing, finance, media, and government. Some clients hire us to help with data strategy, to shape technology roadmaps and find the right talent for their data science team. Others use us as a sounding board on data products, to find the best libraries, models and algorithms. Others ask us buy/build questions, where we pitch in on vendor selection.
